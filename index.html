Email ~ mayankr at berkeley dot edu | dblp, Scholar

I am a third-year CS Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley advised by Prof. Raluca Ada Popa.

From 2018-2020, I was a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research (MSR) India, where I worked closely with Nishanth Chandran, Divya Gupta, Rahul Sharma and Aseem Rastogi on the EzPC project. I did my B.Tech. from the Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University) Varanasi. During my undergrad, I worked with Alexander Chepurnoy, Prof. Takeshi Koshiba, Keisuke Tanaka and Mario Larangeira.
